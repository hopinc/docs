## Overview

Hop is a cloud platform built from the ground up (seriously!) for the modern developer. Everything is architected to be as low-latency and developer-friendly as possible. Focus on your product, not infrastructure.

## Products

### Ignite

Deploy any kind of application. Leave the infrastructure to us.

<Card title="Ignite overview" icon="fire-flame-curved" href="/ignite/overview">
	Ignite provides containers as a service. Think about it like Kubernetes,
	without the hassle and boilerplate that comes with it. Ignite also offers the
	option to attach GPUs to your containers.
</Card>
